Built on purpose, driven by science.

Founded in November 2023 in Cairo, Biosyn Pharmaceuticals is an emerging company with a global mindset — committed to delivering premium-quality, accessible medications where they're needed most.

Our Story

A simple belief, rigorously pursued.

Biosyn emerged from the conviction that quality medication should never be a privilege. We are an Egyptian pharmaceutical company blending scientific rigor, ethical sourcing, and human-centered design to deliver therapies that matter.

We focus where we can make the deepest impact: Pediatrics and Otolaryngology — two areas where formulation precision, safety, and accessibility define the difference between care and cure.

Focused Specialization

Two therapeutic areas. Deep expertise.

Rather than spread thin, we go deep — focusing on the areas where formulation precision and patient safety matter most.

Specialty 01

Pediatrics

Pediatric care demands precision: gentle dosing, palatable formulations, and absolute safety. We design with smaller patients in mind — medications built for them, not adapted from adults.

  • Age-appropriate formulations
  • Pediatric-safe excipients
  • Palatability research
  • Accurate pediatric dosing
Specialty 02

Otolaryngology

Ear, nose, and throat conditions touch millions daily. Our ENT portfolio focuses on nasal care: isotonic and hypertonic sprays, soothing gels, and innovative formulations that bring measurable relief.

  • Isotonic & hypertonic systems
  • Mucosal-friendly excipients
  • Sustained-release gels
  • Allergic rhinitis solutions

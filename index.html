About Biosyn

A new generation of pharmaceutical care, built on purpose.

Founded in November 2023, Biosyn Pharmaceuticals emerged from a simple yet powerful belief — quality medication should never be a privilege. We are an Egyptian pharmaceutical company with a global mindset, blending scientific rigor, ethical sourcing, and human-centered design to deliver therapies that matter.

We focus where we can make the deepest impact: Pediatrics and Otolaryngology. Two therapeutic areas where formulation precision, safety, and accessibility define the difference between care and cure.

Focused Specialization

Two therapeutic areas. Deep expertise.

Rather than spread thin, we go deep. Biosyn focuses on the therapeutic areas where formulation precision and patient safety matter most.

Specialty 01

Pediatrics

From newborns to adolescents, pediatric pharmaceutical care demands precision: gentle dosing, palatable formulations, and absolute safety. We design with smaller patients in mind — because they deserve medications built for them, not adapted from adults.

  • Age-appropriate formulations
  • Pediatric-safe excipients
  • Palatability research
  • Accurate pediatric dosing
Specialty 02

Otolaryngology

Ear, nose, and throat conditions touch millions daily — from chronic congestion to acute infection. Our ENT portfolio focuses on nasal care: isotonic and hypertonic sprays, soothing gels, and innovative formulations that bring measurable relief to real patients.

  • Isotonic & hypertonic systems
  • Mucosal-friendly excipients
  • Sustained-release gels
  • Allergic rhinitis solutions

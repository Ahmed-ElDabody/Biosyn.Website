FAQ

Questions, answered.

What therapeutic areas does Biosyn focus on?
Biosyn focuses on three core therapeutic areas: Pediatrics, Otolaryngology (ENT), and Pulmonology. This focused approach lets us develop deeper expertise and more precise formulations rather than spreading across many categories.
Are Biosyn products approved and regulated?
Yes. All our marketed products are notified with the Egyptian Drug Authority (EDA) and manufactured under strict Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) standards, with multi-stage quality control.
Where are Biosyn products manufactured?
Our products are manufactured in Egypt under GMP-compliant facilities and processes, following international pharmaceutical quality standards.
How can healthcare professionals or pharmacies partner with Biosyn?
We welcome partnership inquiries from healthcare professionals, pharmacies, and distributors. Please reach out through our Contact page and our team will respond within one business day.
How do I report a side effect or product concern?
Patient safety is our priority. If you experience any adverse effect or have a product quality concern, please contact us at Info@biosyn-ph.com or call +20 100 049 2070 so our team can assist and document it appropriately.
